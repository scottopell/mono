    How it works
    • Time passes on its own. Pressure builds. Influence accrues.
    • Release early: tight outcome band, small nudge.
    • Release late: wider variance, higher ceiling, worse floor.
    • Wait too long (pressure maxes out): it releases without you, biased against you.
    • Every release leaves a permanent fault. Geology carries into the next epoch.
