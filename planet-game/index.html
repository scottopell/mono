    How it works

    Events — discrete stresses on your crust. They appear over time and ripen visibly through five stages: hairline, fissure, glowing crack, pulsing, about to rupture. Up to 5 active at once.

    Tap to release. Tap an event before it ruptures and it becomes a permanent terrain feature on your map (a positive ridge). Ignore it long enough and it auto-resolves into a scar (permanent damage).

    Hold to charge. A quick tap spends 10 Will (Normal). Hold for Focused (50 Will, ×2 outcome). Hold longer for Deep (100 Will, ×3). The pie ring shows your charge.

    Stability is derived from your map: features add quality, scars subtract damage. Reach 100 to enter the next epoch — features and scars carry forward, but the stability anchor resets so each epoch has its own goal.
