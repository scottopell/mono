    How it works
    Ridge — a steady release. Lifted into your crust.
    Canyon — a rough release. Carved into your crust.
    Eruption — pressure reached 100 and let go without you. A red glow marks the site. You don't choose where.

    Low pressure → small, tight outcomes. High pressure → bigger swings, higher ceiling, worse floor. Smooth crust feels steadier; scarred crust is a gamble.
