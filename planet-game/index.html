    How it works
    • Time passes on its own. Pressure builds. Influence accrues.
    • Tap the planet where you want to release. Each release costs 10 influence.
    • Positive releases raise ridges; negative ones carve canyons. The terrain remembers your outcomes.
    • The ring around the planet shows scarring — yellow is worn, red is scarred.
    • Virgin crust: tight outcome band, positive bias. Safe bread-and-butter.
    • Scarred crust: wider swing, negative drift — high ceiling, worse floor. Gamble.
    • Low pressure = small swings regardless of where you tap.
    • High pressure = bigger swings. If it maxes out, it releases unforced and biased against you.
    • Stability ≥ 100 → next epoch begins. Geology carries forward.
